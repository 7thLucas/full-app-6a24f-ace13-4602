# Vault — Product Overview

## What Vault Is
Vault is a modern mobile banking application that gives users instant, intuitive access to their financial life. It centralises the three actions people perform most often — checking their balance, managing their payment cards, and reviewing transactions — into one clean, frictionless experience.

## The Problem
Traditional banking apps are cluttered, slow, and hard to navigate. Finding a balance takes multiple taps; freezing a card requires a phone call; understanding spending means scrolling through cryptic, unsearchable transaction lists. The experience has not kept pace with what users expect from their phones.

## The Solution
A streamlined banking interface that surfaces the most important financial information — balance, cards, and transactions — immediately and clearly, with a calm, premium design that feels trustworthy. Every screen has a clear purpose; every action takes one tap.

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Everyday banking customers who want fast, reliable access to their account information on mobile
- **Secondary**: Mobile-first users frustrated with outdated or overly complex banking interfaces

## Core Features
1. **Balance Overview** — Instant, clear display of current account balance and available funds, visible the moment the app opens. No extra taps required.
2. **Card Management** — View and manage debit/credit cards, freeze/unfreeze instantly, set spending limits — all without calling a branch.
3. **Transaction History** — A clean, categorised, searchable feed of all transactions with readable merchant names, dates, and amounts.

## Positioning
- **Category**: Retail / Personal Banking App
- **Differentiator**: Radical simplicity — Vault does three things and does them exceptionally well. No feature bloat, no upsells obscuring the basics.
- **Tone**: Modern, calm, trustworthy, approachable. Confident without being loud. Not cold or corporate; not flashy or overhyped.

## Brand
- **Name**: Vault
- **Tagline**: Banking, Simplified.
- **Primary colour**: Deep Navy `#0f1f3d`
- **Mid Navy**: `#1e3a5f`
- **Accent colour**: Blue `#3b82f6`
- **Light accent**: `#93c5fd`
- **Background**: White / Slate
- **Typography style**: Bold, clean display type for headlines; legible sans-serif for body copy

## Strategic Principles
- **Speed first**: every core action reachable in one tap from the home screen
- **Clarity over comprehensiveness**: show what the user needs, hide what they don't
- **Trust through design**: premium, calm, and transparent UI signals reliability
- **Real-time**: balance and card state always live — no stale data, no manual refresh
- **Full transparency**: every transaction tracked, categorised, and visible
