# Product Overview

## Product Name
(TBD — working title: Modern Banking App)

## What It Is
A modern mobile banking application that gives users a clean, frictionless way to check their account balance, manage their cards, and review transactions — all in one place.

## Core User Problem
Traditional banking apps are cluttered, slow, and difficult to navigate. Users struggle to get a quick read on their finances, manage card settings, or find a specific transaction without wading through confusing menus.

## Solution
A streamlined banking interface that surfaces the most important financial information — balance, cards, and transactions — immediately and clearly, with a calm, premium design that feels trustworthy.

## Target Users
- Everyday banking customers who want fast, reliable access to their account information
- People frustrated with outdated or overly complex banking interfaces
- Mobile-first users who prefer managing finances on their phone

## Core Features
1. **Balance Overview** — Instant, clear display of current account balance and account summary
2. **Card Management** — View and manage debit/credit cards, freeze/unfreeze, set limits
3. **Transaction History** — Clean, filterable transaction feed with categorisation and search

## Positioning
A modern, user-first banking experience: calm, fast, and trustworthy. The app prioritises clarity and ease of use over feature bloat.

## Tone & Brand
Premium, calm, minimal. Trustworthy and approachable — not cold or corporate, not flashy or overhyped. Dark navy and clean white with accent blue as primary palette.

## Scope
- Balance checking
- Card management
- Transaction viewing and history
- Out of scope (v1): loan applications, investment tools, bill pay, peer-to-peer transfers
